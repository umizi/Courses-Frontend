This is Courses.IO

A project for Girls Who Code 2015.

What is Courses.IO?

Courses.IO was created out of a need to organize and plan courses and activities in order to succeed in high school. Many juniors and seniors find themselves in a difficult position every year when they attempt to register for a course that they have a passion or an interest in, only to find that they are unable to take the class due to not meeting certain prerequisites in their freshman and sophomore year. In order to combat that predicament and to encourage younger students to keep organized and prepare for the future, we created Courses.IO.

About Izzy Cao

Izzy is a rising senior at Hunter College High School. She had the privelege of having her school designate required prerequisites for all her classes, so now she wants other students who don't have that option to still be organized and to take all the classes that they want. At the same time, she wishes that she had planned her summer extracurriculars in a more organized fashion after spending several listless summers thinking about how to spend her summer. In her free time, she is an active member of her school's theater and arts community, often performing in choir concerts or directing theatrical performances.
