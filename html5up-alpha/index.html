Introducing the newest way to clean up
your high school life and plan ahead

Courses.io is a new way to plan your high school path, using simplified design and a clean, responsive interface to allow you to organize your needs and goals.

Plan your high school life

Enter all your courses and map out your schedules according to your needs and your hobbies.

Track your progress

Keep track of your prerequisites and any other requirements needed to take the classes you want.

Interact with others

Keep in touch with your classmates. Give and get support and advice by connecting to their accounts.

Find and give support

Ask for advice from your teachers in shaping your high school career. Reach out and network with all of your peers.

Why choose Courses.io?

No other web service out there serves to give you, the high school student, exactly the tools you need to organize your high school career.

Made by students, for students.

Driven by the need to plan our high school careers, we sought to create a website for future students to avoid the same problems we had.
